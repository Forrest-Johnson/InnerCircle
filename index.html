Meet TreeRover: A Tree Planting Robot

Monday, September 14, 2015 ◎

TreeRover is a tree planting robot with one goal: to plant trees for you!

In the not too distant future, planting trees will be done autonomously. At least, that's what Iota Enterprises, a startup company based in Victoria, BC believes. Co-founders Tyler Rhodes and Nick Birch are in the process of bringing TreeRover to the masses in order to combat the ever increasing threat of deforestation facing forests around the world.

As of October 9, 2015 the Iota team reached 132% of their IndieGoGo goal of $5,000 CAD.

Apple Event

Wednesday, September 9, 2015 ◎

Today, Apple is expected to release two new iPhones models, the 6s and 6s Plus. With the flurry of rumored new features surrounding the new iPhones, it can be difficult to decipher what rumors are credible, and which ones are just nonsense.

I would bet that the new iPhones will include the following new features:

  • Force Touch Display

  • Improved iSight and FaceTime Cameras

  • Rose Gold Color Option

Also rumored for todays event will be the long awaited refresh of the Apple TV, which has not received a significant update since March 7, 2012.

Today, at 10 AM PST, the world will finally know what Apple has in store for the future.
