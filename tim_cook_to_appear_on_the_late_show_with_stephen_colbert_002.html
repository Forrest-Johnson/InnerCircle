Tim Cook to appear on The Late Show with Stephen Colbert

Friday, September 11, 2015

In a tweet today, Stephen Colbert announced that Apple CEO Tim Cook will be appearing as a guest on The Late Show next Tuesday.

“Siri, put Apple CEO Tim Cook on my calendar for Tuesday, Sept. 15. Oh, and come up with some questions to ask him.”

With iOS 9 debuting the next day, it will be interesting to hear what they discuss.
